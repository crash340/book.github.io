About The Book

In the book titled “Partners of the Heart” by Vivien T.Thomas he writes about his life before even pursuing or entering the medical field.He mostly grew up in Nashville city where he was raised with his other four siblings.His parents both worked to provide for the family.The mother was working as a tailor and taking care of the kids.Also, the father working as a carpenter and providing for the other expenses.At a young age Vivien was quiet smart.He graduated with more credits than he needed for highschool.Working was something that had importance in his life but never over took the power of education.Vivien started to work when he was thirteen a relatively young age.He later on finds a job at the hospital as a janitor and this is where his journey begins to become a heart surgeon.

Characters

The main characters in the book are Dr.Blalock and his assistant Viven Thomas.They both are smart and they collaborated for such a long period to figure out the Blue baby syndrome.There were many ups and downs but they still accomplished what they needed to do everyday.Out of all his colleagues he started to trust Viven more due to the teaching and experience he gave him at the hospital everyday, and that's why he was chosen to be part of the extremely difficult surgery at the end.
